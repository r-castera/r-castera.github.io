Since November 2024, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Moroccan Center for Game Theory with Professor Rida Laraki.


From October 2021 to October 2024 I was a PhD student at Université Grenoble Alpes, in the POLARIS team of Grenoble’s Computer Science Laboratory (LIG). I was also part of the Fairplay team located at ENSAE Paris. My PhD was advised by Patrick Loiseau and Bary Pradelski. You can download my PhD thesis here .


I hold an Engineering Degree in Applied Mathematics from Ecole Polytechnique, as well as a MSc in Optimization from Paris-Saclay University and Institut Polytechnique de Paris. Prior to that, I graduated with a BSc in General and Applied Mathematics from Claude Bernard Lyon 1 University. Here is my complete CV.
